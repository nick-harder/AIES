AI in Energy Systems

This course provides master's students with practical knowledge and skills for applying Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) techniques specifically tailored to energy systems. Building directly upon prior Python programming experience and electricity market modeling (such as merit-order models), students will learn to implement AI methods for forecasting, optimization, anomaly detection, and reinforcement learning (RL) in realistic energy scenarios.
