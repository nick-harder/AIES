AI in Energy Systems

This course equips master’s students with practical tools and applied knowledge to tackle real-world challenges in modern energy systems using Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML). Building on foundational skills in Python and prior exposure to electricity market modeling (e.g., merit-order models), students will learn how to develop, implement, and evaluate AI techniques tailored to forecasting, optimization, classification, and decision-making in energy applications.
